We, Together

Introvert is a term Arnab would readily use to describe himself. As he stepped into college, he wasn’t looking for ‘fun’ but desired to delve deep into the mysteries of history and get a degree. While most people got busy getting to know their classmates, he handpicked his own small group and chose to remain within that. Rashmi considered herself a born leader so within a month of joining college she became the class representative. Compulsively talkative, eager to make new friends, willing to participate in extra-curricular activities, good in academics and active in campus politics, Rashmi soon became a name everyone knew. Poles apart, the two remained just classmates sharing a cordial relationship for the longest time till things began to change. The ‘leader’ realized how vulnerable she could be and found the strongest shoulder in none other than the shyest boy of the class. A cordial relationship developed into a deep friendship as she laid bare her deepest secrets to someone who was always willing to listen. She talked and he listened for days on end and they fell in love. Six years have passed by but he is still not tired of listening to the garrulous girl. Endless fights, innumerable misunderstandings, hundreds of trips and gazillions of restaurant hopping later, they decided to spend the rest of the life doing the same things.

Wedding Events

Come January 16, 2017 and the quietest boy in town says his vows to the most talkative one. It’s a union with a difference and since they have been blessed with a large number of friends, they plan to celebrate the wedding ‘big, fat’ style at Neelkantha on January 16 and Bijoy Majil on January 18.
